Claims Process

Simple, fast, and hassle-free claim settlement process

How to File a Claim

Follow these simple steps for quick claim settlement

1

Immediate Intimation

Call our helpline immediately after the incident. Our team will guide you through the initial process.

Within: 24 hours of incident
Required: Policy number, incident details
2

Document Submission

Submit the required documents online or visit our nearest branch. Our team will help you prepare the complete documentation.

Mode: Online upload or branch visit
Time: Within 7 days of intimation
3

Claim Assessment

Our expert assessors will evaluate your claim. For health claims, we work directly with hospitals for cashless treatment.

Timeline: 48-72 hours
Updates: SMS and email notifications

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the claims process

How long does it take to process a claim?

+

Most claims are processed within 3-7 working days after all required documents are submitted. Emergency claims and cashless treatments are processed within 24-48 hours.

Can I submit documents online?

+

Yes, you can upload all required documents through our online portal or mobile app. This speeds up the processing time significantly compared to physical submission.

What is cashless treatment facility?

+

Cashless treatment allows you to get medical treatment at our network hospitals without paying upfront. We settle the bills directly with the hospital after pre-authorization.

What if my claim is rejected?

+

If your claim is rejected, you'll receive a detailed explanation. You can appeal the decision by providing additional documentation or contacting our customer service team for clarification.

Can I track my claim status online?

+

Yes, you can track your claim status in real-time using our online portal, mobile app, or the tracking tool on this page. You'll also receive SMS and email updates.

Is there a time limit to file a claim?

+

Yes, claims should be intimated within 24 hours of the incident and documents submitted within 30 days. However, we may consider claims beyond this period based on valid reasons.

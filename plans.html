Insurance Plans

Choose the perfect coverage for your unique needs

Health Insurance

Health insurance provides financial coverage for medical expenses incurred due to illness, injury, or hospitalization.

Coverage Includes:

  • Hospitalization expenses (room rent, nursing, doctor fees)
  • Day-care procedures & surgeries
  • Pre and post hospitalization costs
  • Critical illness cover (cancer, heart attack, kidney failure, etc.)
  • Maternity and newborn care (in some plans)

Benefits:

  • Cashless treatment in network hospitals
  • Family floater options for the entire family
  • Tax benefits under Section 80D

Life Insurance

Life insurance provides financial protection to your family in case of death or disability, ensuring long-term security.

Types:

  • Term Plan: High coverage at low premium
  • Whole Life: Lifetime coverage with maturity benefits
  • Endowment Policy: Insurance + savings
  • ULIP: Insurance + investment linked to market

Benefits:

  • Financial security for dependents
  • Wealth creation with savings/investment options
  • Tax benefits and loan facility

Motor Insurance

Motor insurance provides coverage against risks related to vehicles such as accidents, theft, and damages.

Types:

  • Third Party Insurance (Mandatory)
  • Comprehensive Insurance (Own vehicle + third-party)
  • Own Damage Cover

Coverage:

  • Accidents, theft, fire, natural calamities
  • Third-party injury, death, or property damage

Benefits:

  • Mandatory by law (RTO requirement)
  • Repair/replacement expenses covered
  • No Claim Bonus (NCB) discount

Travel Insurance

Travel insurance ensures financial protection during domestic or international trips against medical and travel-related risks.

Coverage:

  • Medical emergencies abroad
  • Loss of passport, baggage, or documents
  • Flight delay or cancellation
  • Personal liability cover

Benefits:

  • Protection against high foreign medical costs
  • Peace of mind during travel
  • Compensation for losses & delays

Commercial Insurance

Commercial insurance safeguards business properties, assets, and operations against various risks.

Coverage:

  • Fire and burglary damages
  • Property insurance (factories, shops, offices)
  • Machinery/equipment breakdown
  • Business interruption losses

Benefits:

  • Ensures business continuity
  • Protection against natural disasters & theft
  • Legal liability coverage

Business Insurance

Business insurance protects entrepreneurs and companies from operational, legal, and financial risks.

Coverage:

  • Commercial property insurance
  • Workers compensation insurance
  • Cyber liability insurance
  • Professional liability cover

Benefits:

  • Protects employees and assets
  • Reduces financial burden from lawsuits
  • Provides long-term business stability

Liability Insurance

Liability insurance covers legal expenses and damages if a third party is harmed by your business or services.

Types:

  • Public liability insurance
  • Product liability insurance
  • Professional indemnity insurance

Benefits:

  • Covers court/legal expenses
  • Protects brand reputation
  • Required for businesses with customer exposure

Employee Benefits Insurance

Employee benefits insurance provides group coverage for employees offered by employers.

Types:

  • Group health insurance
  • Group term life insurance
  • Accidental coverage
  • Pension & retirement benefits

Benefits:

  • Boosts employee satisfaction & retention
  • Tax benefits for employers
  • Financial security for employees

Marine / Transit Insurance

Marine insurance protects goods and cargo during transit by sea, air, or road.

Coverage:

  • Damage/loss during transportation
  • Import/export shipments
  • Risks of piracy, war, and natural disasters

Benefits:

  • Essential for global trade
  • Protects against large financial losses
  • Ensures smooth logistics operations

Property Insurance

Property insurance protects residential or commercial property from risks like theft, fire, and disasters.

Coverage:

  • Fire, theft, vandalism
  • Earthquake, flood, storm damages
  • Damage to building & contents

Benefits:

  • Compensation for repair/reconstruction
  • Protects real estate assets
  • Peace of mind for owners

Home Insurance

Home insurance provides financial protection to homeowners against risks that may damage their property or belongings.

Coverage:

  • Fire, burglary, earthquake, flood
  • Loss or damage of household contents

Benefits:

  • Protects house and belongings
  • Financial stability in case of damage

Fire Insurance

Fire insurance covers losses caused by fire-related accidents.

Coverage:

  • Fire, lightning, explosion
  • Accidental fires

Benefits:

  • Repair or rebuilding cost covered
  • Protects commercial & residential assets

Crop Insurance

Crop insurance provides financial protection to farmers against crop losses due to unforeseen events.

Coverage:

  • Floods, drought, cyclones
  • Pest or disease outbreaks

Benefits:

  • Stability for farmers’ income
  • Encourages agricultural growth

Retirement / Pension Insurance

Pension insurance provides a steady income after retirement to ensure financial independence.

Types:

  • Immediate annuity
  • Deferred annuity
  • Pension-linked investment plans

Benefits:

  • Financial independence post-retirement
  • Regular lifelong income
  • Tax benefits under pension rules

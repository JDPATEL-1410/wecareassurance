Insurance Plans

Choose the perfect coverage for your unique needs

Life Insurance

Life insurance provides financial protection to your family in case of death or disability.

Plans

  • Term Plan (High coverage, low premium)
  • Endowment Policy (Insurance + savings)
  • ULIP (Insurance + investment)
  • Whole Life Plan (Lifetime cover)

How to Claim?

  1. Inform the insurance company immediately.
  2. Submit death/disability certificate and policy documents.
  3. Fill claim form and provide ID/address proof.
  4. Claim is processed and amount is transferred.

General Insurance

General Insurance covers Health, Motor, Travel, Property, Fire, Home, Liability, Group, Corporate, Professional etc.

Health Insurance

Covers hospitalization, surgeries, critical illnesses.

How to Claim?

  1. Inform insurer & get claim intimation number.
  2. Submit hospital bills & reports.
  3. Cashless or reimbursement settlement done.

Motor Insurance

Covers accidents, theft, and damages to vehicles.

Types:

  • 2 Wheeler Insurance
  • 4 Wheeler Insurance
  • Commercial Vehicle Insurance
  • Passenger Vehicle Insurance
  • Miscellaneous Vehicle Insurance

How to Claim?

  1. File FIR (if theft/accident).
  2. Inform insurer within 24 hours.
  3. Surveyor inspects vehicle, repair/replacement approved.

Travel Insurance

Covers medical emergencies abroad, baggage loss, trip delays.

How to Claim?

  1. Contact insurer helpline immediately (24x7 worldwide).
  2. Submit boarding pass, bills, and loss proof.
  3. Claim settled directly or reimbursement given.

Property / Fire / Home Insurance

Covers fire, burglary, natural disasters, and damages.

How to Claim?

  1. Notify insurer and fire authority immediately.
  2. Submit claim form, fire report, and bills.
  3. Surveyor inspects & approves compensation.

Liability Insurance

Covers legal liability to third parties.

How to Claim?

  1. Notify insurer with claim details.
  2. Submit legal documents / court notices.
  3. Insurer provides legal and financial support.

Group Insurance

Provides health, accidental, and term coverage to groups of employees or members.

How to Claim?

  1. Employer/member informs insurer.
  2. Submit claim form with documents.
  3. Cashless or reimbursement settlement processed.

Corporate Insurance

Covers businesses for property, liability, cyber, and operational risks.

How to Claim?

  1. Report loss/damage with evidence.
  2. Submit claim form and relevant documents.
  3. Survey & settlement done by insurer.

Doctor & Professional Insurance

Protects professionals (doctors, lawyers, consultants) against negligence, errors, or malpractice claims.

How to Claim?

  1. Inform insurer of case filed/complaint received.
  2. Submit documents, FIR (if applicable).
  3. Insurer covers legal defense and compensation.

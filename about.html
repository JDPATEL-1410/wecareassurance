About WE CARE ASSURANCE

Your trusted partner in insurance since 2003

Our Story

Founded in 2003, WE CARE ASSURANCE began with a simple mission: to provide reliable, affordable insurance coverage with a personal touch. What started as a small family business has grown into one of India's most trusted insurance providers.

Over the past 22 years, we have protected over 6,000 families and businesses, settling claims worth millions with a 98% settlement ratio. Our commitment to transparency, customer service, and innovative solutions has made us a leader in the insurance industry.

Today, we continue to evolve and adapt to meet the changing needs of our customers, leveraging technology while maintaining the personal relationships that have always been at the heart of our business.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted insurance partner, providing comprehensive protection and peace of mind to every family and business we serve.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional insurance solutions through innovation, integrity, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction, ensuring financial security for all.
